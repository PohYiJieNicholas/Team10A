About

The Curio project (trycurio.com) is a web-based, affordable educational platform designed to teach undergraduate students programming, particularly in web development, UI/UX design, and AI/ML. The platform leverages the user's own smartphone or laptop via Web-Bluetooth and can access device sensors and internetbased services. Curio promotes rapid learning and easy project sharing, facilitating quick iterations and widespread dissemination of student work.

Problem

The Curio robot consists of basic components - two motors, wheels, a battery, a control board, and a wooden frame and is meant to be low cost to be affordable by schools and students alike. But the platform can feel limiting at times and people regularly comment on the robot speed, or are confused by its design (e.g., which way is the front?). How might we design a new improved Curio? You will explore these questions using a development kit, called Smartibot, allowing your team to explore designs ideas quickly.
